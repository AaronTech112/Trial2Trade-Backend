Trading Rules Guide
Trial2trade

A Simple Guide to Help You Pass the Challenge & Get a funded Account

1. Payout Conditions

To successfully pass the Trial2trade Challenge and receive a funded account, you must:

  • Achieve 15% profit on the trial account without breaking any rules.

Profit Split Policy:

  • First payout: 85% profit share to you, 15% to Trial2trade (service fee).
  • Subsequent payouts: 95% profit share to you, 5% to Trial2trade.

This ensures fairness while rewarding consistent, disciplined traders.

2. Drawdown & Account Protection

A. Daily Drawdown Limit – 4% (Fixed)
You cannot lose more than 4% of the initial account balance in a single day.
Example: $10,000 account = $400 max daily loss.

B. Maximum Drawdown – 10% (Balance Based)
You cannot lose more than 10% of the initial account balance
Example: on a $10,000 dollar account you cannot loose more than $1,000 overall...

 Why? This protects your capital and ensures sustainable growth.

3. Risk Management Rules

  • Max Positions per Asset: Not more 3 open positions on the same assets|pair at a time.
  • Max Risk per Trade: do not lose more than 2% of your initial account balance on single trade
  • E.g On a $10,000 account loosing $200 dollars on A single trade is a breach, but opening two positions and risking 1% each ($100 ×2= $200) is not a breach.
  • Minimum Trading Days: At least 20 trading days must be recorded before qualifying for funding.
  • News Trading:  Allowed.

4 Scalping Rules

Opening and closing a trade within two (2) minutes, is strictly not allowed.
Any trade held for less than two minutes will be classified as scalping and considered a breach of trading rules.

5 Account Inactivity / Dormancy Rule

If a trader does not place any trades for 45 consecutive days, the account will automatically become dormant and invalid.
Once an account becomes dormant, all progress made on that account is considered void and cannot be reactivated.
This ensures active participation and fair use of the Trial2trade trading environment.

Final Notes

⚠ Every rule listed above is a hard rule. Any breach = immediate disqualification.
Trial2trade will not be responsible for account losses due to breaches. We are here to reward disciplined and skilled traders who take their trading journey seriously and are ready to level up.
