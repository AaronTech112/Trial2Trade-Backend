Trial2Trade Trading Rules Guide

A simple guide to help you pass the challenge and get a funded account.

1. Payout Conditions

To successfully pass the Trial2Trade Challenge and receive a funded account, you must:

  • Achieve 15% profit on the trial account without breaking any rules.
  • Trial2Trade purchases a prop firm account from a reliable firm, giving you full control.
  • Trial2Trade does not take any profit share from your funded account.

This ensures fairness while rewarding consistent, disciplined traders.

2. Drawdown & Account Protection

Daily Drawdown Limit - 4% (Fixed)

You cannot lose more than 4% of the initial account balance in a single day.

Example: $10,000 account = $400 max daily loss.

Maximum Drawdown - 10% (Balance Based)

You cannot lose more than 10% of the initial account balance.

Example: $10,000 account = $1,000 max overall loss.

This protects your capital and ensures sustainable growth.

3. Risk Management Rules

  • Max positions per asset: not more than 3 open positions on the same pair.
  • Max risk per trade: do not risk more than 2% of the initial balance per trade.
  • Example: On a $10,000 account, losing $200 on one trade is a breach, but two trades risking 1% each is allowed.
  • Minimum trading days: at least 20 trading days are required before funding.
  • News trading: allowed.

4. Scalping Rules

  • Opening and closing a trade within 5 minutes is strictly not allowed.
  • Any trade held for less than 5 minutes will be considered a breach.

5. Account Inactivity / Dormancy

  • No trades for 15 consecutive days means the account becomes dormant.
  • Dormant accounts are invalid and cannot be reactivated.

6. Account Activity Rules

  • You must place at least one trade within 5 days of purchase.
  • Failure to do so will result in account breach and inactivity.

Final Notes

  • All rules are strict. Any breach results in immediate disqualification.
  • Trial2Trade is not responsible for losses due to rule violations.
  • We reward disciplined, serious traders who are ready to level up.
