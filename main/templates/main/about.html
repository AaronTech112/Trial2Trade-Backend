About Trial2Trade

Our Story

Trial2Trade is a forex challenge firm with a clear mission — to buy prop firm accounts for traders who pass their challenge.

We are proudly affiliated with some of the industry's top-tier firms — FundedNext, The5ers, FTMO, and FundingPips — giving traders access to real opportunities and real capital once they prove their skills.

It all started on May 19th, 2025, with just $1,700 in capital and a bold vision: To break down the financial barriers young and upcoming traders—especially in Africa—face when trying to purchase prop firm accounts.

We saw the hustle.
We saw the talent.
But most of all, we saw the struggle.

Too many skilled traders were held back not because they lacked ability, but because they couldn't afford to play the game.

So we changed the rules.

Instead of spending hundreds of dollars on prop firm evaluations, traders could now buy a Trial2Trade challenge account for a fraction of the price. And if they passed — we bought them a full prop firm account.

Today, just a few months in, Trial2Trade has proudly helped over 50 traders step into the big leagues — purchasing over $500,000 worth of prop firm accounts for rising talents across the continent.
